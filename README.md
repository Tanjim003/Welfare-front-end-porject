## Overview

E-Welfare Bangladesh is a community-driven initiative that bridges the gap between volunteers, medical professionals, farmers, and those in need across all 64 districts of Bangladesh. Our platform facilitates various social welfare programs and community support services.

## Features

- **Community Engagement**: Connect volunteers with local initiatives
- **Healthcare Access**: Medical professional registration and free health camps
- **Agricultural Support**: Modern farming programs for farmers
- **Environmental Initiatives**: Tree plantation and animal welfare programs
- **Food Distribution**: Community food share programs
- **Donation Portal**: Secure platform for financial and material donations
