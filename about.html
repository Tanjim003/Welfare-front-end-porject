Our Story

Founded in Dhaka during the 2018 flood crisis, E-Welfare began as a small group of volunteers distributing food and medicine to affected communities. Today, we've grown into a nationwide network serving all 64 districts.

Our work is guided by the principle that sustainable change comes from empowering local communities while addressing immediate needs.
